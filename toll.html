The Toll

The Toll is the final book in the Arc of a Scythe series, and in this book, scythes Anastasia, Lucifer, and Faraday dance with death in a desperate bid to save the world from Goddard. The Thunderhead is the only one who can truly save them, yet, it is forbidden to intervene in these matters. Instead, the Thunderhead must speak through the Toll, to bring all those he has exiled together, and to save the world on its behalf.

Synopsis

Scythes Anastasia and , Lucifer are both on the run from Goddards goons, as Goddard gains power over the world. The Thunderhead has deemed everyone unsavory, so people are helpless, as for the firts time in their life, they have had to do things for themselves. Everyone turns towards Greyson Tolliver, the only person who can communicate with the Thunderhead. Meanwhile, Scythe Faraday is desperately trying to reach the founders' failsafe. The world is on the brink of destruction, and everything rests on the shoulders of four people - Greyson, Scythe Faraday, Citra, and Rowan.

In my opinion...

In my opinion, this is my favourite book of all time. The interesting thing about this series is that it actually gets better as the series progressed. This book was filled with action, and had me sitting on the edge of my seat the whole time. When the series ended, I was absolutely shocked. Everything was wrapped up. I couldn't think of any questions. I was completely satisfied. And for the first time ever, I was happy with the last book of a series.
