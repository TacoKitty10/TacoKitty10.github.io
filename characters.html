Citra Terranova/Scythe Anastasia

Citra Terranova is, arguably, the main character in this series. She is a brave, smart, compassionate girl, who despises scythes. She detests gleaning, but that's what makes her such a good scythe. She gleans with compassion, and respects those who she gleans.

Rowan Damisch/Scythe Lucifer

Rowan Damisch is the second lead in the Arc of a Scythe series. He is cunning and smart, and relies on his wits and motivation to become one of the scariest scythes the world has ever seen - capable of killing other scythes if he wishes. Though Rowan seems scary, and his actions are often questionable, he remains loyal to Citra, and always has the best of intentions.

Scythe Faraday

Scythe Faraday is Rowan and Citra's mentor. He was the one who selected them to be apprentices. Scythe Faraday is a kind, wise person, whom both Citra and Rowan love dearly. He is instrumental to both their futures.

Back to top

Scythe Curie

Scythe Curie is a wise, kind, compassionate mentor, who introduces Citra to a new side of gleaning, and how important her job actually is. She truly loves Citra, and would sacrifice her life for her. Scythe Curie is similar to Citra's grandmother - giving her wisdom and protection in the dangerous world of the scythes.

Back to top

Scythe Goddard

Scythe Goddard is the antagonist of this series. Power-hungry, conniving, and charming, Goddard uses his wits and sweet-talking skills to win scythes over. He promotes gleaning as a sport, and completely disregards the old-guard ways. He will be the one to lead the world to destruction, but hey, at least he's the leader, right? The scariest thing about Goddard is the fact that he actually believes what he teaches - he's lied so much, even he believes in himself. Totally deranged.

Back to top

The Thunderhead

The Thunderhead is the benevolent ruler of the world. It was originally known as 'the cloud' until it become a sentient AI being, and resumed control of the world. However, the Thunderhead never has, and never will control humans, as he treasures them, similar to a parent. It simply protects them from harm, and ensures that they are happy and content. The Thunderhead, however, is forbidden from interfering in scythe affairs. But as it is all-seeing and all-knowing, it doesn't miss the growing tension between the scythes, and can accurately predict whatever misfortunes that are about to occur. It must work around the rules to protect its beloved humanity from total destruction.

Greyson Tolliver/The Toll

Greyson is a devoted follower of the Thunderhead. His dream was to become a Nimbus agent and serve the Thunderhead. But why, you ask. Well, the reason for this devotion, or, as some might call it, love, is due to the fact that the Thunderhead is like a parent to Greyson. His biological parents were always away, and they never really cared about their kids, so the Thunderhead was the one who always asked Greyson how his day was, and so on. Little did Greyson know, this bond between him and the Thunderhead was one of the most important things in the future, and he would save many lives. Greyson is a kind, smart, and simple person, and he saves many of our beloved scythes.

Scythe Rand

Scythe Rand is a devoted follower of Goddard's. She's quite psychopathic, and extremely cunning and skilled. However, she does have a weakness - Tyger Salazar. Rand lives life for herself, and if anyone tries to stop her, they'll find themselves on the wrond edge of a sword.

Jerico Soberanis

Jerico, or more commonly known as Jeri, is a Madagascan. When the sun is out, Jeri is a woman, but when the clouds cover the sun, Jeri is a man. Jeri's an accomplished sailor, and a beloved captain. Smart, quick-witted, strong and self-sacrificing, Jeri is one of the key characters of this series, but only comes in the later books.
