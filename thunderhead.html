Thunderhead

Thunderhead is the second book in the Arc of a Scythe trilogy. In this book, Scythe Anastasia embraces her new role as a scythe, and Scythe Lucifer is now on the run for hunting and murdering corrupt scythes. Disaster is brewing as dead enemies rise from the ashes, reborn anew. Scythe Faraday travels in a race against time to find the one thing that could save them all, while the Thunderhead can only sit and watch as its precious world faces its biggest threat yet.

Basic Info

  • Book #: 2
  • Pg count: 512
  • Rating: 10/10

Synopsis

In this second book, Scythe Anastasia begins fulfilling her duties as a scythe, gleaning with compassion with her mentor, Scythe Curie alongside her. Meanwhile, Scythe Lucifer prowls among the streets, sniffing out corrupt scythes, and giving them chances at redemption. As old enemies are reborn anew, the scythes must work together to save the world, as the Thunderhead is powerless to stop the world's imminent destruction. The scythes must use the law to bring their enemies down, as the Thunderhead desperately searches for loopholes in its rules as both race against time to save the world. Scythe Faraday sets out on his quest for the founders' fail-safe, as it seems as if that is all that stands between Goddard and the world.

In my opinion...

In my opinion, though I really enjoyed Scythe, this book was even better. The twists and turns in this book - absolutely unbelievable. I stayed up way later that I should've, and often fell asleep dreaming about these new, shocking revelations, vowing to read more the next day. This book was truly spectacular, and through utter desperation, death and despair, our sycthes stay strong.
